The 3DS


The 3DS is the follow up to the classical DS line. The handheld has had multiple versions such as the: 3DS, New 3DS, 3DS XL, New 3DS XL, 2DS, and the 2DS XL. The 3DS family is fully backwards compatable with all Nintendo DS games allowing for a massive library of games that people of all ages can enjoy!





The Switch


The Switch is nintendos latest console. With this you have the power of both a handheld and a console in the power of one device. Playing on the go and hooking up to any TV is simple as can be. The switch has an ever growing library and a large choice of colors to choose for the joycons.





The Switch Lite


The Switch Lite is Nintendo's latest version of the Switch. The Switch Lite comes in three colors: Yellow, Teal and Dark Gray. This version allows you to play all of your favorite switch games but just on the go! For those that are always on the go and want to save money it's the best option!
